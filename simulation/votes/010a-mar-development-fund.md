**Outcome:** ENACTED — 3-1, 148M-68M. Establishes the MAR-Gate Hestia Development and Border Cooperation Fund: €120M over 5 years (€24M annually), Fiscal Key-funded by Gate Members, jointly governed by a MAR-Gate Joint Committee with equal representation and consensus-based decision-making. MAR contributes equivalent in-kind cooperation. Fund covers border infrastructure modernisation, customs/migration capacity-building, and legal migration pathway infrastructure.

FRA voted NO on principled grounds (bilateral leverage preference) but cannot block on population threshold. The same DEU+ITA+NOR minimum coalition carries this as it carried 001, 007, and 008.

**MAR Anchor Hestia conversion confirmed:** With both 008 and 010-A enacted, MAR formally converts from conditional abstention to active YES on all Anchor Hestia instruments.
