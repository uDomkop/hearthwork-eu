**Outcome:** ENACTED — 4-0 with POL abstaining. The partnership language rewrites Regulation 001 Art. 2.5 to require all third-country cooperation agreements to be founded on partnership of equals, binding capacity-building commitments, sovereignty protection, no-criminalisation provisions, and labour rights standards. Applies prospectively to all new Gate Hestia third-country cooperation agreements.

Notably, FRA voted YES despite its hard constraint against MAR becoming a Gate obligation — the language imposes procedural obligations on how Gate agreements must be *framed*, not new substantive obligations on specific states.
