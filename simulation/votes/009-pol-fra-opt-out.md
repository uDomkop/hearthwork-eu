**Outcome:** ENACTED — 3-0, 164M (abstentions not counted against). The opt-out mechanism amends Regulation 001 to add Article 1.8 (Financial Solidarity Option). States may elect a 15% Fiscal Key surcharge in lieu of physical relocation, subject to Council approval by double majority and a 2-simultaneous-state cap. Mandatory 2-year review applies.

**The political shift:** POL's Round 1 firm NO on 001 is converted. Regulation 001 as twice-amended (by 005 and 009) now has the support of DEU (via abstain, not oppose), FRA, ITA, POL, and NOR — a substantially broader coalition than the original 3-2 enactment. The relocation mechanism is more politically durable, if somewhat less physically coercive.

ITA's YES was the decisive move. ITA accepted that a binding financial obligation with a cap preserving physical solidarity for at least 3 of 5 states satisfied ITA's core red line.
